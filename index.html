What is Skene

A DAW, a modular studio, and a stage rig in one

One app for the whole show. Compose music, design sound, build reactive visuals, and run the lights — all in a single node-based workstation for Mac, iPad, and iPhone.

Multi-touch expression

Play every note with your fingers

A grid of multi-touch note pads built for MPE. Press and hold, then slide a finger sideways to bend the pitch and up or down to sweep the filter cutoff — each note shaped on its own, so chords and leads come alive under your hands.

Features

Built for the stage and the studio

Every tool works together in real-time, whether you’re composing or performing live.

Node Graph at the Core
Patch instruments, effects, modulation, visuals, and even lighting as connected nodes. Every parameter is a typed port you can route anything into — LFOs, envelopes, audio levels — including feedback loops a linear rack can’t express.
Node Graph
MIDI Tracks & Piano Roll
Full MIDI track workflow with a clip-based arranger and piano roll editor. MPE support adds per-note pitch bend, pressure, and Y-axis slide — draw curves directly in the piano roll to shape expression over time.
MPE
Audio Tracks
Multi-track audio recording and playback with a clip-based arranger. Process tracks with the full built-in and AUv3 effects library, and set clips to loop or play through once.
Built-in Instruments
Analog Synthesizer — 3 oscillator polyphonic synth with MPE support, vocoder, and unison. Analog Drum Synthesizer — classic 70s analog drum machine with per-pad synthesis. Drum Sampler and multi-sample Sampler with MIDI control and cue points.
Built-in Effects
Freeverb reverb, stereo delay, saturator, compressor, Warm waveshaper, Juno-style chorus, phaser, Neural Amp Modeler for guitar & bass, and a full filter library: ladder, resonant, formant, and Butterworth high/low pass.
AUv3 Plugin Hosting
Load any AUv3 instrument or effect plugin directly into the node graph. Third-party synths, samplers, and processors become first-class nodes, patchable alongside Skene’s built-in devices.
AUv3
MIDI Effects
Per-clip MIDI effect chain with arpeggiator, groove, quantize, and velocity randomizer. Effects animate live in the piano roll as you play, so you can see the pattern as it unfolds.
Live Performance & Scenes
Launch clips and whole scenes on the beat from the Arranger and remix a set as you play. Loop sections live, toggle lights and effects, and add mutators on the fly — all mappable to your hardware controllers via MIDI Learn for hands-on performance.
Live
GPU Visuals
Metal-powered visuals that live in the same graph as your sound. Feed audio and MIDI into shaders, particle systems, and feedback effects for graphics that react to the music in real time.
Visuals
DMX512 Lighting
Drive stage lighting and DMX fixtures — LED bars, moving heads, haze — over Art-Net or USB. A light’s parameters become node ports, so the same signals that shape your sound can shape the room.
DMX
Tuner & Recorder
Real-time pitch tuner showing detected note and cents deviation. Quick audio recorder for capturing ideas instantly, without stopping your session or leaving the current view.
Cross-Device & iCloud Sync
One project, every device. Native on Mac, iPad, and iPhone, with iCloud sync so you can start a session on one device and pick it up on another exactly where you left off.
