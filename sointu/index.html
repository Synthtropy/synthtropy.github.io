Features

Built for the stage and the studio

Every tool works together in real-time, whether you’re composing or performing live.

AUv3 Instrument Hosting
Load any AUv3 instrument plugin directly into the node graph. Third-party synths and samplers become first-class nodes, patchable alongside Sointu’s built-in instruments.
AUv3
AUv3 Effect Hosting
Insert AUv3 audio effect plugins anywhere in the signal chain. Mix third-party processors with the built-in delay, saturator, compressor, and warmth nodes.
AUv3
MIDI Tracks & Piano Roll
Full MIDI track workflow with a clip-based arranger and piano roll editor. MPE support adds per-note pitch bend, pressure, and Y-axis slide — draw curves directly in the piano roll to shape expression over time.
MPE
Built-in Instruments
Analog Synthesizer — 6-voice polyphonic synth with MPE support, vocoder, and unison. Analog Drum Synthesizer — classic 70s analog drum machine with per-pad synthesis. Drum Sampler and multi-sample Sampler with MIDI control and cue points.
Built-in Effects
Freeverb reverb, stereo delay, saturator, compressor, Warm waveshaper, Juno-style chorus, phaser, and a full filter library: Moog ladder, Roland TB-303, Korg 35, resonant, formant, and Butterworth high/low pass. All patchable as nodes.
Audio Tracks
Multi-track audio recording and playback with a clip-based arranger. Overdub mode, punch-in recording, live audio bounce to recorder, and iCloud sync across all your devices.
Node-Based Effect Stack
Build signal chains visually by connecting instruments and effects as nodes. Typed ports enforce signal compatibility — audio, MIDI, and control signals route cleanly through the graph.
MIDI Effects
Per-clip MIDI effect chain with arpeggiator, groove, quantize, and velocity randomizer. Effects animate live in the piano roll as you play, so you can see the pattern as it unfolds.
Tuner & Recorder
Real-time pitch tuner showing detected note and cents deviation. Quick audio recorder for capturing ideas instantly, without stopping your session or leaving the current view.
